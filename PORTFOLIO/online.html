    GENERAL INFORMATION ON ADMISSION

    1.1. The school reserves the right to deny a student who is married and pregnant. Students who marry during the school year are required to withdraw.

    1.2. A student who is involved in any pending civil, criminal or administrative case/s shall present upon enrollment police clearance, municipal court clearance, and clearance from the Court of First Instance, and clearance from the Provincial Fiscal. The school does not admit any student who is convicted in any criminal case even during pendency of an appeal.

    1.3. The school reserves the right not to admit, drops, dismisses, and readmits any student who makes false statements in the application for admission.

    1.4. Students who were dropped from or who left school without valid reason, or were suspended for more than 20% of the total days of attendance for the school year will not be admitted the following school year.

    1.5. Students with failure not made up or have been refused admission in other schools for disciplinary reasons shall not be admitted.
